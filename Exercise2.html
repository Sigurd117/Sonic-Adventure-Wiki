Sonic Adventure is video game made on the Dreamcast by SEGA.

Published on January 31, 2024 by Giovanni Lara

This is Sonic's first true transition into 3D as a platformer. It was one of SEGA's sixteen launch titles for the console, and SEGA wanted to provide the player with the same thrill of speed Sonic was founded on.
